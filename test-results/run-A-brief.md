# Signals Brief — 2026-03-16

## Palette Direction
Overcast spring — warm enough to hint at the season turning but veiled, diffused, nothing direct. Think muted greens and soft grays with humidity in them, like looking at new growth through fog. The air is heavy and damp at 93% humidity; colors should feel slightly waterlogged, saturated but never bright.

## Layout Energy
Monday energy: structured, purposeful, no wasted space. But at 11.7 hours of daylight and spring lengthening, there's enough breathing room that it shouldn't feel oppressive — more like a clean desk at the start of the week. Tight grid, clear hierarchy, functional rhythm.

## Tension
St. Patrick's Day is tomorrow — festive, anticipatory — but the palette wants to be overcast and muted, and the new moon is pulling everything toward darkness and restraint. The designer should let the holiday warmth push *through* the gray rather than overpower it: green that glows faintly under cloud cover, not a parade. Hold the quiet against the celebration.
