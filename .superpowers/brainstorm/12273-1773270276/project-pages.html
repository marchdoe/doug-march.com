How deep do individual projects go?

When someone clicks a project, what do they get?

A

Full case study page

Each project gets its own page — problem, approach, outcome, visuals, tech stack. The kind of depth that closes a client deal or impresses a hiring team.

B

Lightweight detail — then link out

A short description + key details, then a link to the live product, GitHub, or wherever the real thing lives. Low maintenance, lets the work speak for itself.

C

Both — depends on the project

Big projects (Spaceman, major client work) get full pages. Side projects and experiments get a quick description + link. Flexible by content.
