## Claude's Rationale

The Split archetype orchestrates "sequential discovery"—a fixed left identity panel (name, role, Tigers score, Deng quote) provides navigational certainty and Friday's purposeful structure, while the scrollable right panel unfolds content vertically, honoring the brief's "forward motion paused at threshold" mood. Cool spring green (H:125°) displaces three consecutive warm-green days, delivering freshness through cooler register without saturation excess. IBM Plex Mono as the heading font encodes the Claude Opus tech signal structurally: monospace letterforms in every heading create texture you *feel* rather than decoration you notice. Perfect Fourth scale (1.333) with 1.55 body line-height balances Friday's productivity against the new moon's introspection. Every color is hue-tinted throughout (no pure black/white), honoring the ambient-light constraint while maintaining WCAG AA accessibility. The type pairing fuses Radiohead's angular precision (monospace strictness) with Tobin Sprout's humanist warmth (Source Sans 3's open letterforms) and Wet Leg's permission for subtle imperfection—the entire system feels perfectly calibrated for a day suspended at the threshold.
