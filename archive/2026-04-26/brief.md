## Claude's Rationale

The brief mandates the Bhagavad Gita quote as an 88% hero moment in uninterrupted visual quiet—a directive that only the Poster archetype structurally protects. The color palette—cool cerulean primary (210°) tinted neutral with muted sage (120°) as the sole accent—honors both the spring luminosity and existential weight of the quote, avoiding all forbidden hue zones while respecting the "fresh greens restrained" directive. A single 9-step stone neutral scale (S:~8–9%) provides sophisticated near-neutrality; sage appears only in method tag pills and the hero accent rule, signaling craft through precision rather than saturation. Spacing discipline enforces the Sunday rhythm: 96px section gaps, 40px row padding, zero card grids—density at ~20% of typical portfolio density, allowing the quote to breathe undisturbed. The hero's asymmetric radial gradient (center 58% / 40%, not dead-center) embodies the waxing gibbous 76% illumination, brightest upper-right rather than full-moon drama.
