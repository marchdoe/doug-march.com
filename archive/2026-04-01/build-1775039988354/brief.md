## Claude's Rationale

The terracotta primary hue (22°) avoids the recent amber–slate runs, landing in warm rust territory that encodes spring clarity through maximum contrast—deep near-black base (#0D0A08) against bright warm-white text (#F0EAE3), with zero mid-tone backgrounds as the structural law derived from the full moon's over-lit clarity signal. Syne (geometric, subtly idiosyncratic) paired with Work Sans (slightly condensed, code-adjacent) resolves the brief's core tension: technically precise yet carrying indie rock sincerity without affectation. The Scroll archetype with embedded Specimen beats (the Einstein quote at full display scale) respects the spring-renewal mood and Wednesday's structured pulse—beats oscillate between generous 96px padding (daylight openness) and dense 48–64px (Wednesday information density). The full moon signal mandates crispness: zero border-radius, zero shadows, hairline borders only, all surfaces resolving to deep dark or bright text with no intermediate gray. Burnt orange (#C95220) is the sole chromatic presence, paired with a single 2px sand-colored rule above the footer—Easter atmospheric restraint, one breath only.
