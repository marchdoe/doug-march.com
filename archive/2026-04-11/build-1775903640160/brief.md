## Claude's Rationale

This palette is built on H:218° — a pre-dawn indigo sky, cooler and higher than any morning teal, chosen to describe the sky itself rather than light landing on surfaces. The rust accent (H:15°) provides the warm horizon tension, while pale gold (H:43°) is reserved for the three specified uses: leaderboard rank, quote border, and nav indicator. Space Grotesk's angular precision carries Radiohead's compressed geometry while Work Sans breathes with My Morning Jacket's organic warmth. The Scroll archetype governs all spacing — 96px section beats, 720px max content width, generous vertical breathing room that rewards unhurried discovery rather than scanning.
