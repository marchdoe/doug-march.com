## Claude's Rationale

This system embodies the Visual Specification precisely: steel-slate blue neutrals (hue 212°) build a page that feels lit from within a cloud rather than overhead. The cold verdigris accent (#2C7D5A) appears exactly once as a structural token — the borderSignal — honoring the spec's mandate of one chromatic punch. Space Grotesk at the extreme end of the Perfect Fifth scale (81px–120px) gives the specimen its architectural spine, while JetBrains Mono at 16px makes every index row feel like terminal output — a system being read. The dark base with suppressed midtones reflects the new moon's instruction: highlights are earned, not given.

The semantic layer maps to specimen logic: `bg` is the deep page field (#0D1922), `bgCard` is the slightly lifted card plane, `text` carries the luminous-but-not-white primary (#E2E8ED), and `textMuted` at neutral-500 (#475970) carries all the metadata — scores, dates, section labels — that the specimen treats as contextual noise rather than signal.
