## Claude's Rationale

Spring green at 108° sits in genuinely fresh territory — grass-blade measured rather than triumphant, which mirrors the brief's held tension between renewal and Thursday flatness. The warm amber accent (35°) does exactly one job: editorial punctuation against the green-gray field. Syne's architectural geometry carries Radiohead's controlled angularity while Work Sans at 300 weight holds Wet Leg's slightly irreverent ease. The Perfect Fourth scale gives each size a noticeable jump — appropriate for Scroll's cinematic beat-by-beat pacing. No shadows, no rounded corners, no compromise: spring arrives through color and breathing room alone.
