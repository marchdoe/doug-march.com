## Claude's Rationale

This design system prioritizes **dark-mode clarity with extreme typographic contrast** to match the full moon's isolation in a night sky. The choice of 215° cool hue (shifted from sky blue into stone/archival territory) and the 67px display type with tight leading enforce the Scroll archetype's cinematic pacing while embedding Specimen's expressive scale gaps. JetBrains Mono as the body font is unconventional but structurally honest—it encodes Doug's technical identity into every label and paragraph, avoiding decorative choices in favor of pragmatic code-aesthetic restraint. The spring green accent (#2FA865) appears exactly once, above the Rumi quote, consolidating the planting metaphor into a single seed-dot moment. Every other color decision derives from the cool neutral scale, honoring the brief's demand for restrained, archival feeling rather than vibrant spring abundance.
