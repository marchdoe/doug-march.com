## Claude's Rationale

The design system responds to a scroll-based, spacious Sunday afternoon—the brief's explicit direction for "Scroll with Specimen moments" anchors a vertical flow where content breathes and white space becomes structural. The color palette lands at hue 70° (warm olive-mustard), deliberately positioned outside the recent 32–58° amber-stone run, resonating with early spring's muted yellow-greens and the 89% waxing gibbous moon overhead. This creates a page that feels lit from within, not through artificial warmth. Typography pairs DM Serif Display (literary, stroke-rich headings) with Work Sans (geometric, unhurried body), separated by deliberate scale contrast (Perfect Fourth 1.333) and letter-spacing encoding: headings pull in at -0.025em, body breathes out at 0.04em, mirroring the musical warmth of My Morning Jacket and Guided by Voices. The neutral scale (50–900) is derived from hue 70° with minimal saturation, ensuring the singular accent color (olive-gold at 70°, S:70) has real presence when it appears. Secondary sage green (#5C8A62) appears exclusively on the golf leaderboard's "Live" indicator—a single spring gesture, nothing more.
