## Claude's Rationale

The Gallery Wall archetype demanded a complete break from axis-based layouts — electric teal (188°) on near-black grounds creates a cyberpunk-gallery energy that reads as curation, not template. Syne's geometric, slightly irregular stroke modulation at large sizes reads as architecture; Work Sans pairs neutrally without competing. The semantic token layer maps raw neutrals and accent values to purposeful roles (bg, bgCard, bgFeatured, bgQuote, text, textSecondary, textMuted, accent, signal, win, loss) so components can describe intent rather than raw color. The Perfect Fourth scale (1.333) gives enough editorial drama for the large featured block without becoming unwieldy in the compact score rows.
